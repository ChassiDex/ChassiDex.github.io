Bacillus subtilis

prokaryotic facultative-anaerobic gram-positive endosporulating

Growth Characteristics

Optimal temperature is 37-degree celsius. The doubling time is 30 min1. The optimum pH is 72. It can divide asymmetrically to form an endospore. This sporulation occurs under stressful, nutrient-starved conditions. The endospore is resistant to unfavorable environmental conditions like extreme pH, heat, radiation, etc3. B. subtilis grows in certain glucose and nitrogen sources. Some of the growth mediums are: C-minimal medium4 M9 minimal medium 5 Spizizen minimal medium6 Long Version Nutrient Agar7 2X SG Media8
